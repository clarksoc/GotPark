Got Park App Manual

Getting Started!

Introduction:

Welcome to GotPark the mobile app that lets you reserve a parking spot anywhere you want!

Reserving Your First Spot!

Now that you have signed up, you can start reserving parking spots~

To do so, follow these steps:

  • Open the "Locate Nearby Parking Spots" Tab
  • Find Your Preffered Parking Spot
  • Select That Parking Spot and You Will Be Redirected to the "Add Parking" Tab Automatically
  • Enter in a "Building Code" Number
    • This will be a unique value for each building you decide to park by
  • Select Your Desired Parking Time-Slot
    • This can be 1-hour or less ($4), 3-hours ($8), 10-hours ($12), or 24-hours ($20)
    • Your first 3 parkings of each month are on the house!
  • Enter in your Cars Plate Number
    • This plate number should match the one you entered for your profile
  • Enter in the Hosts Suite Number
    • This should be the room number of the building you are parking at

Parking Receipts

Now that you have reserved your first parking spot, we can move onto viewing your parking receipt

There are two ways of viewing your receipts:

  • Most Recent Parking Receipt
    • To view your most recent parking receipt, select the "View Recent Parking Receipt" tab
    • This will display all the information of that receipt (Date/Time, Plate Number, Building Code, Suite Number, and Duration)
  • All Parking Receipts
    • To view all of your parking receipts, select the "View All Parking Receipts" tab
    • This will display every parking receipt in a list, which you can select any to view all the information of the selected receipt (Date/Time, Plate Number, Building Code, Suite Number, and Duration)

User Profile

Now that you have mastered parking with GotPark, there is only one thing left to learn, how to update your profile!

To do so, select the "Update Profile" tab on the home screen

You can update your name, email, password, phone number, credit card information and plate number

Once you have finished updating, just hit the save button and you are all set to park again!
